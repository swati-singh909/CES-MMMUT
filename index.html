F.A.Q

  • Is the there any entry fee to be a part of this contest?

    No, the event is free of cost.

  • How much time will I get to submit the website?

    You’ll get at least 4 – 5 days to submit the website.

  • Do I have to make a complete dynamic website?

    You just need to make a template i.e. a single page website.

  • How many rounds are there in the event?

    There will be 2 rounds in the contest , the first one , is a quiz based on web development technologies and the second one ,is to make a single page application.

  • Why should we participate in this event?

    Participating in such events improves your coding skills and help you to explore new technologies.

  • How many questions will the quiz comprise of and on which topic will they be from?

    There will be a total of 30 question based HTML,CSS and Javascript ,Git and Github.

  • What are the prerequisites to participate in the event?

    You just need to have a basic knowledge of Html and Css to participate in the event.

  • I do not know much about Git and Github, can I still participate?

    Yes, of course . You don’t need to worry about it, our team will orgainse a session that will help you understand Git and Github.
